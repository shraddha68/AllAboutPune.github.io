Famous Restaurants in Pune!

Hotel Shreyas

Hotel Shreyas on Apte Road in Pune is a welcoming establishment known for its comfortable accommodations and attentive hospitality. Conveniently located in the heart of the city, it provides easy access to key landmarks and business centres. The hotel's restaurant offers a diverse menu, serving delicious meals. Whether you're starting your day with a hearty breakfast or enjoying a late-night dinner, the restaurant provides a satisfying culinary experience. With well-appointed rooms and a cozy dining ambiance, Hotel Shreyas caters to both business and leisure travellers, ensuring a memorable stay in Pune.

Hotel Vaishali

Hotel Vaishali, a Pune landmark, stands as a testament to the city's rich history. Established years ago, it combines tradition with modern comfort, offering a unique stay experience. The hotel's culinary offerings are a highlight, with a diverse menu featuring local and international delights. From classic Pune cuisine to global flavours, the restaurant at Vaishali caters to discerning palates. Known for its warm hospitality and central location, Hotel Vaishali continues to be a preferred choice for travellers, seamlessly blending history with contemporary amenities.

Barbeque Nation

BBQ Nation in Pune is a top choice for barbecue enthusiasts, offering a unique dining experience. Known for its interactive grill tables, patrons can savour a variety of grilled delights right at their table. The restaurant boasts a diverse menu with a wide range of kebabs and mains, catering to different tastes. The lively ambiance and live grilling add to the overall dining excitement. BBQ Nation in Pune is a popular destination for those looking to enjoy a flavourful and interactive barbecue feast.

Malaka Spice

Malaka Spice in Pune is a popular dining destination, celebrated for its diverse Pan-Asian cuisine. With a menu featuring Thai, Vietnamese, Malaysian, and Indonesian flavours, Malaka Spice provides a unique and flavourful dining experience. Known for its attention to detail and artistic presentation, the restaurant attracts food enthusiasts seeking an authentic taste of Asia. Whether indulging in Thai curries or Vietnamese pho, Malaka Spice stands out as a culinary gem in Pune.

Durvankur Dining Hall

In Pune, Durvankur Dining Hall stands as a cultural hub, embracing Maharashtrian heritage in its warm ambiance. The traditional decor adds to the authentic experience of indulging in a classic Maharashtrian thali. Renowned for its diverse offerings, the menu includes regional delicacies like puran poli and pithla bhakri. A visit to Durvankur is not just a culinary delight but a cultural immersion, inviting patrons to savour both the flavours and traditions of Maharashtra.
